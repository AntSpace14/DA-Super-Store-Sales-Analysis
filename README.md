An **interactive Excel dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance using the Global Superstore dataset.  
It highlights **trends, product performance, regional contributions, customer behavior, and profit patterns**, helping stakeholders make informed, data-driven decisions.

💼 The dashboard serves as a **strategic solution** by clearly showcasing high-performing areas, identifying loss-making segments, and revealing growth opportunities.

---

## 🧩 Business Problem

Global Superstore encounters multiple operational challenges, including:

- Fluctuating **sales and profit** across regions  
- Losses in some categories due to **heavy discounting**  
- Limited visibility into **top and underperforming products**  
- Lack of clarity in **customer purchasing behavior**  
- Difficulty identifying **seasonal trends**  
- No centralized reporting system for **decision-making**

### ✔ Dashboard Objectives:
- Track sales, profit, and discount patterns  
- Identify high-performing regions and customer segments  
- Highlight low-profit categories and optimize pricing strategies  
- Analyze seasonal sales trends  
- Enable data-driven decision-making

## 💡 Key Insights

### 📈 Overall Performance
- **Total Sales:** $22.97M  
- **Total Profit:** $286K  
- **Orders:** 5,009  
- **Customers:** 793  

### 🛒 Category Insights
- **Technology** generates the highest sales and profit  
- **Furniture** has lower profitability due to heavy discounts  
- **Office Supplies** perform steadily with lower margins  

### 🌎 Regional Insights
- **West Region (32%)** leads in total sales  
- **Central Region (22%)** has the lowest profitability → requires pricing review  

### 👥 Customer Segments
- **Consumer (50%)** is the top revenue contributor  
- Corporate: 31%  
- Home Office: 19%  

### 🏆 Top Products
- Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier  
- Fellowes PB500 Binding Machine  

Most top-performing products belong to **Technology** and **Office Supplies**.

### 📆 Sales Trends
- Peak months: **March, September, November**  
- Indicates strong seasonal or promotional sales patterns
